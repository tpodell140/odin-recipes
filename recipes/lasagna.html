Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 box Lasagna Noodles
  • 2 Bell Peppers
  • 2 small Zucchini
  • 1 Yellow Squash
  • 1 28oz can Crushed Tomatoes
  • 1 15oz conatainer Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese (shredded)
  • Parmesan Cheese (shredded)
  • 1 egg

Instructions

  1. Boil lasagna noodles according to package directions
  2. Chop all veggies to bite size pieces. A food processor can be used for the zucchini and squash
  3. Saute onion and garlic until onion is translucent
  4. Add peppers and saute until sof
  5. Add remainign vegetables, cook for approximately 10 minutes
  6. Mix ricotta cheese and egg in a bowl
  7. Layer noodles across the bottom of a baking pan
  8. Add 1/3 of the veggie sauce mixture. Top with 1/3 of the ricotta cheese and 1/3 of the mozz and parmesan
  9. Add another layer of noodles. Repeat the layering 2 more times
  10. Bake for 30 minutes in a 350 degree oven
