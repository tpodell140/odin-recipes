Roast Chicken

Ingredients

  • Whole chicken
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Herbs de Provence
  • Paprika

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 475 degrees F
  2. Pat chicken dry and remove backbone with kitchen shears
  3. Turn chicken over and press down to break the wishbone. Lay chicken flat so that legs and breast are as spread out as possible
  4. Season chicken with salt pepper herbs de provence and paprkia. Drizzle with olive oil
  5. Roast for 45 minutes at 475, using convection if available
  6. Rest for 10 minutes
